Making the world better, one plant at a time

The EIFK is a portable hydroponic system that allows any user to easily and nutritiously grow their own food. Our kit uses a simplistic system of tubes to constantly circulates water to allow the system to water the plants on its own. Our system also lets the user know if the plants are dehydrated and need more water. Our product produces fresh, healthy, and organic vegetables. The only way you truly know if your food is organic is if you grow it yourself. Most stores use pesticides on their plants which can make them harmful and unhealthy to the user. Since our kit is used inside, it does not need harmful sprays, so our produce remain healthy. Our product is affordable and we are a non-profit organization. Half of our profit helps us keep our product cost low and the other half goes to places that can't afford our product. The EIFK comes with a rechargeble battery powered motor, just add water. Our system also provides a low LED light during the night to keep your plants healthy and growing.

What is Hydroponics?

Hydroponics is a way growing plants without soil. All you need is water, sand and nutrients for the plant. You can grow a variety of plants, which makes this cheap and easy.

You don't need to constantly add water. The system we've created constantly circulates the water througout the plants. This system allows plants to grow all year long. It also allows it to grow indoors. An added bonus, growing indoors gets rid of pests. You can also show our app a picture of the plants to show you if it is dehydrated and needs more water.
