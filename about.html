How EIFK was created

Coronovirus hit us in 2019, and a lot of people had trouble going to the supermarket. We met eachother on a code-a-thon program a couple of years ago. So after covid hit, we immediatly got on a zoom call and discussed the issue. We did some research and realized how unhealthy vegetables from the supermarket are. And that organic food has pest spray that is harmful to humans. We had friends and family that were having trouble eating, and we came to an agreement that no one should be hungry. We are currently trying to change America, and we are then going to change the world. We wanted to create a product that was affordable, simple, and helped changed the world. And that is how EIFK was formed.

Why did we did it?

We believe that no one should be hungry and everyone should live a long and healthy life.
